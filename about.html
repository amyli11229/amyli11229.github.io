About

I am a designer and researcher working at the intersection of visual arts, human-computer interaction, and new media. My practice explores how technology can enhance human expression and create meaningful interactive experiences.

Through a combination of creative coding, user-centered design, and experimental media, I investigate the boundaries between digital and physical interactions. My work often questions how we perceive and engage with computational systems in everyday life.
