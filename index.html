About

AMY YIQIN IS A PAINTER, DESIGNER, AND MULTIMEDIA ARTIST BASED IN VANCOUVER

Her work explores the intersection of visual arts, human-computer interaction, and new media. She is interested in how technology can enhance human expression and create meaningful interactive experiences.

Through a combination of creative coding, user-centered design, and experimental media, Amy investigate the boundaries between digital and physical interactions. Often questions how we perceive and engage with computational systems in everyday life.
