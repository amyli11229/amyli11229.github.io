Procedural Poetry Funhouse

EML Experimental VR Project Unreal Engine 5, Figma, Blender, Procreate, Meta Quest VR

An experimental VR experience combining procedural environments, visual poetry, and immersive storytelling. The project explored how interactive spatial design and motion-based navigation could create emotional and narrative experiences within virtual reality.

As a UI/UX designer on the project, I contributed to interaction design, interface planning, and immersive experience development while collaborating closely with developers in an agile workflow. I also worked on visual assets, 3D modeling, and animation using Blender and Procreate.

A major focus of the project involved balancing artistic experimentation with usability and motion comfort in VR environments. Through iterative prototyping and testing, the experience was refined to create smoother navigation and more intuitive interaction flows for users.

Key contributions

  • Designed interaction and user experience flow for VR environments
  • Collaborated with developers using agile workflows
  • Created visual assets, 3D models, and animations
  • Iterated on immersive interactions to reduce motion discomfort
  • Assisted with prototyping and environmental storytelling
